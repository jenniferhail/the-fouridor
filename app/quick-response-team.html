We make it easy.

South Central Indiana’s Quick Response Team is made up of people from several cooperating organizations, including One Southern Indiana, Duke Energy, The Port of Indiana-Jeffersonville, and the River Ridge Commerce Center. We pull together to eliminate any obstacles and expedite processes to help get your business up, operational, and profitable as quickly as possible. As one site consultant recently declared, "I can't believe it was this easy!"

Matching

We’ll help you find the ideal facility or greenfield site that meets your needs and makes your path to profitability easy and fuss-free. Once we know your business needs, we’ll know what to do.

Supporting

If you need state and local support, we’re here to help you Identify the tax and incentive programs that help your business succeed, as well as help to expedite any required permitting.

Touring

We like to show off our area, so we happily Arrange and host site, building, and community tours to give you the lay of the land and an in-depth experience of the Fouridor and all of its assets.

Guiding

We’ll provide professional assistance through your entire selection process, from incentives guidance and permitting assistance, we’ll guide you even long after the ribbons have been cut.
